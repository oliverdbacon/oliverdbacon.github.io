Outside of music, I go by Oliver Bacon. I currently live in Tacoma WA, and attend the University of Puget Sound. I am a computer science student and intend on majoring in artificial intelligence. Other than making music I enjoy relaxing and working on projects. I have a fascination with oscilloscopes, and I own a 760a Tektronix Vectorscope. I also really enjoy cars. I drive a 1992 Subaru SVX which has made an appearance in the canvas on "Right Now" on my EP "Mirror" and also a few of my earliest albums that can be found on my Soundcloud.

Throughout my life I have always enjoyed making music. I took piano lessons when I was 7 and learned how to record and use Garageband to make tunes. I eventually retired from Garageband to use Fl Studio in highschool. I still use Fl for the majority of my work. I have only ever collaborated with WhoCreatedUs? but I plan on doing more collaborations in the future. If you are interested in collaborating check out my Business Inquiries page.

To catch up on what I am doing, make sure to follow my socials! I post WIP tunes on my YouTube and update my Instagram whenever I have an upcoming release. Whether you are just stopping by, or you have been a fan, I really appreciate you for listening to my music. It's what I'm passionate about, and knowing that just one person enjoys listening makes me happy.
